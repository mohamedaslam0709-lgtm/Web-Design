About Taz'een Academy

Welcome to Taz'een Academy, a dedicated Quran learning academy committed to nurturing a deeper understanding and connection with the Holy Quran. Our mission is to provide accessible, authentic, and high-quality Islamic education to students of all ages and backgrounds, fostering spiritual growth and lifelong learning. At Taz'een Academy, we combine traditional teachings with modern technology to create an engaging and flexible learning environment. Our experienced and qualified instructors are passionate about helping students master Tajweed, memorization (Hifz), recitation, and the interpretation (Tafsir) of the Quran. Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps or an advanced learner seeking refinement, our tailored programs cater to every level of expertise. We believe that learning the Quran is not just about recitation but also about embodying its teachings in daily life. Through structured lessons, personalized guidance, and interactive sessions, we aim to empower our students with the knowledge and skills to strengthen their faith and character. Join us on this transformative journey as we strive to build a global community of learners united by their love for the Quran. Together, let's illuminate hearts and minds with the timeless wisdom of The Almighty's words.

As mentioned before, Taz'een Academy is an independent organization which aims to help everyone who has a desire to learn Quran. In addition to this, our objectives also include building moral ethics of our students and teaching them about the Islamic way of life. With innovative one on one sessions from our expert Quran teachers, you will be learning how you can become a better Muslim and make your life exactly according to the commandments of Islam. With fluency in the English language, our teachers make the best use of one on one online sessions and teach you at a pace that matches exactly your learning ability. In the end, Taz'een Academy aims to make everyone understand the teachings of the holy Quran through online Quranic sessions, with the comfort of their home, at a time that suits them best. E learning is now need of the time and being accepted worldwide. Success of online training depends on the proficiency of the teacher to keep his/her student engaged and focused. We hire trained and qualified staff who are expert in their domain and have online teaching experience.
